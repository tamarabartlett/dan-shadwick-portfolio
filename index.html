Daniel Shadwick

A.F.F.I & T.I

Daniel begabn his journey in skydiving in 2005 with a tandem, quickly becoming hooked on the sport. Since then, he has earned his Advanced Freefall Instructor rating, Tandem Instructor Rating, and Coach Rating with the USPA. He is passionate about both bringing more skydivers into the sport and teaching them skills and safety. Daniel currently works at Mile Hi Skydiving Center in Longmont, Colorado as an A.F.F.I and a T.I. In his free time, he spends his time freeflying or taking canopy courses to perfect his swoop.
